---
title: "Group metadata by invite"
api: "GET /group-invitation-metadata"
description: "Returns group metadata through the invitation link"
---

## Overview

This method returns the group metadata with all group information and its participants through the invitation link.

---

## Attributes

### Header

<ParamField header="Client-Token" type="string">
  Account security token
</ParamField>

### Query Parameters

<ParamField query="url" type="string" required>
  Group invitation link. E.g.: https://chat.whatsapp.com/KNmcL17DqVA0sqkQ5LLA5
</ParamField>

---
