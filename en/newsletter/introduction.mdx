## Overview

In this topic you will learn about all available methods for channel management.

**What are channels?** WhatsApp Channels are similar to Facebook pages, offering a space to share information, news, announcements, and other content related to a specific topic or entity. These channels can be created by a variety of entities, from football teams to streaming service companies, and allow users to follow and engage with content of their interest.

<Info>
It's important to emphasize that the pattern used by WhatsApp for channel IDs always includes the suffix **@newsletter**.
</Info>
