## Overview

This method enables the automatic call rejection option on your API. When enabled, all voice calls received by the number connected to the API will be automatically rejected.

---

## Attributes

### Header

<InstanceId />
<Token />
### Body

<ParamField body="value" type="boolean" required>
  `true` to enable automatic call rejection or `false` to disable
</ParamField>
