## Overview

Method responsible for sending a video to your status. Remember that statuses disappear after 24 hours.

<Info>
Maximum video size for status: 10MB.
</Info>

---

## Attributes

### Header

<ParamField header="Client-Token" type="string">
  Account security token
</ParamField>

### Required

<InstanceId />
<Token />

<ParamField body="video" type="string" required>
  Video link or Base64
</ParamField>

### Optional

<ParamField body="caption" type="string">
  Video caption
</ParamField>

---

## Request Body

```json
{
  "video": "https://example.com/video.mp4",
  "caption": "caption text"
}
```
