## Overview

In this topic you will learn about all available methods for privacy settings.

WhatsApp **privacy settings** are a set of options and features that allow users to control who can access their personal information and interact with them in the messaging app. These settings aim to protect user privacy and offer control over information sharing. The main elements of privacy settings include the ability to define who can see your **profile picture**, **last seen** information, and **read receipts**.
