## Overview

This method is responsible for deactivating a community.

<Info>
Deactivating the community will not delete your groups, but will remove them from the community.
</Info>

---

## Attributes

### Header

<ParamField header="Client-Token" type="string">
  Account security token
</ParamField>

### Path

<ParamField path="communityId" type="string" required>
  Community ID
</ParamField>
