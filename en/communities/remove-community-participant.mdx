This method is responsible for removing participants from a community.

<InstanceId />
<Token />
### Required

<ParamField body="communityId" type="string" required>
  Community ID. Can be obtained from the List communities API
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="phones" type="array" required>
  Array with the phone numbers of participants to be removed
</ParamField>
