title: "Assign categories"
api: "POST /business/categories"
description: "Assigns categories to the WhatsApp Business profile"
---

## Overview

This method is responsible for assigning categories to the WhatsApp Business profile.

<Info>
Maximum of **3 categories** (minimum 1). Values must be IDs or labels returned by the [List categories](/en/business/available-categories) API.
</Info>

---

## Attributes

### Header

<ParamField header="Client-Token" type="string">
  Account security token
</ParamField>

### Required

<ParamField body="categories" type="array" required>
  Category IDs or labels
</ParamField>

---

## Request Body

```json
{
  "categories": ["RESTAURANT", "FINANCE", "629412378414563"]
}
```
