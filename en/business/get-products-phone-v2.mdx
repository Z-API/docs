## Overview

With this method you can get the products from a WhatsApp Business catalog of any number, whether your own or someone else's. This version uses POST with pagination via body.

---

## Attributes

### Header

<InstanceId />
<Token />
### Path

<ParamField path="phone" type="string" required>
  WhatsApp Business phone number
</ParamField>

### Body

<ParamField body="nextCursor" type="string">
  Pagination token to fetch more records
</ParamField>
