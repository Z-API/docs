## Overview

This method is responsible for updating the company address on the WhatsApp Business profile.

---

## Attributes

### Header

<ParamField header="Client-Token" type="string">
  Account security token
</ParamField>

### Required

<ParamField body="value" type="string" required>
  Business address. Send empty to remove
</ParamField>

---

## Request Body

```json
{
  "value": "Endereço da empresa"
}
```
