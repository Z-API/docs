## Overview

This method is responsible for updating the company websites on the WhatsApp Business profile.

<Info>
Maximum of **2 websites** per account.
</Info>

---

## Attributes

### Header

<InstanceId />
<Token />


<ParamField header="Client-Token" type="string">
  Account security token
</ParamField>

### Required

<ParamField body="websites" type="array" required>
  Website URLs. Send an empty array to remove
</ParamField>

---

```json
{
  "websites": ["https://example.com", "https://example2.org"]
}
```
