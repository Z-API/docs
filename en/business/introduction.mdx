## About WhatsApp Business

We now have new APIs for **WhatsApp Business**:

- Create or edit a product
- Get my product catalog
- Get product catalog from another business number
- Get product data by ID
- Delete a product by ID
- Manage labels and collections
- Configure company data

<Warning>
All new APIs **only work** on WhatsApp Business, and are also **only available** for WhatsApp Business using **"Multi Device"**.
</Warning>
