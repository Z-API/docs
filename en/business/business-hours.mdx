## Overview

This method is responsible for updating the business hours on the WhatsApp Business profile.

---

## Attributes

### Header

<InstanceId />
<Token />


<ParamField header="Client-Token" type="string">
  Account security token
</ParamField>

### Required

<ParamField body="timezone" type="string" required>
  Timezone
</ParamField>

### Optional

<ParamField body="mode" type="string">
  Operating mode: `specificHours`, `open24h` or `appointmentOnly`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="days" type="array">
  List of days with business hours (dayOfWeek, openTime, closeTime)
</ParamField>

---

## Request Body

```json
{
  "timezone": "America/Sao_Paulo",
  "days": [
    {
      "dayOfWeek": "MONDAY",
      "openTime": "08:00",
      "closeTime": "12:00"
    }
  ],
  "mode": "specificHours"
}
```
